# Design Context: RallyHub

### Users
- **Customers**: Active badminton players. They need to find and book courts quickly, often on mobile. They value speed, visual clarity, and trust in payments.
- **Owners**: Small to medium business owners. They need to monitor court status and revenue at a glance. They value efficiency and clear data visualization.
- **Admins**: System operators. They need to manage the ecosystem, audit transactions, and approve requests. They value density and control.

### Brand Personality
- **Voice**: Direct, Energetic, Reliable.
- **Personality**: High-Performance, Clean, Modern-Sporty.
- **Emotional Goals**: Confidence (in booking), Excitement (to play), Control (for owners).

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone**: Modern-Sporty with "Precision" accents.
- **Colors**: Anchored by the `linear-gradient(to right, #004E43, #00CE98)`.
- **Theme**: Adaptive. Dark-theme preferred for the Landing/Customer experience (energetic), Light-theme options for data-dense Admin/Owner dashboards.
- **Principles**: 
    - **Rhythm over Repetition**: Avoid endless identical grids.
    - **Purposeful Space**: Use generous padding to separate sections, tight grouping for related info.
    - **OKLCH Precision**: All colors derived from OKLCH for perceptual consistency.

### Design Principles
1. **Direct Path to Booking**: The customer's primary job is booking. Minimal friction.
2. **Status Clarity**: Use semantic colors and clear labels for court status (Banking, Cancel, Complete).
3. **Visual Punch**: Use the brand gradient for high-impact moments (Hero, Primary buttons), not everywhere.
4. **Data Density with Breathing Room**: Dashboard information should be dense but never crowded.
